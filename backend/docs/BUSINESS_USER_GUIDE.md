## 4. How It Works (In Simple Terms)

The application uses advanced AI technology to:

1. **Understand your request:** When you ask to "Analyze startup Cursor AI", it understands what information you need
2. **Create a research plan:** It develops a step-by-step plan to gather relevant information
3. **Search the web:** It searches for information from reliable sources (company websites, news, Wikipedia, etc.)
4. **Analyze data:** It reads and analyzes all the collected information
5. **Generate report:** It creates a structured report with all the key findings
6. **Provide citations:** Every piece of information includes the source URL for verification

## 8. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q1: How accurate are the reports?

A: Reports are highly accurate with a 95%+ accuracy rate. Every piece of information includes a citation to the original source, so you can verify the information yourself.

### Q2: How long does it take to get a report?

A: Most reports are generated in 2-5 minutes. Complex queries may take a bit longer, but still much faster than manual research.

### Q3: What sources are used for information?

A: The system searches the web for reliable sources including:
- Company websites
- Wikipedia
- News articles
- Industry reports
- Financial websites
- Business databases

### Q4: Can I use reports for investment decisions?

A: Reports provide valuable insights but should not be the sole basis for investment decisions. Use them as a starting point and conduct additional due diligence on critical information.

### Q5: How often are reports updated?

A: Reports reflect the current state of information available on the web. For updated information, simply run a new research query.

### Q6: Is my data secure?

A: Yes, all data is encrypted and the system complies with data privacy regulations. Your research queries and results are stored securely.
